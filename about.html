About Me

My name is Lightlia and I am an artist and writer. I currently in Oregon, and like to write and make art in my free time. I take many inspirations from other media such as Homestuck, Star Wars, and Slavic Folklore. My philosophy is that I will not post anything that is not finished beforehand. For example, I will only publish stories that are completly finished, even if I publish a story chapter by chapter.
